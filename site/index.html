Goobits, a hardware random number generator

goobits turns ambient microphone noise into random bits that are available for users to request on this site or over a public API. Also hosted here are statistical tests of archived random bytes, as well as a beacon service that provides public, hash-chained and verifiable randomness. Learn more about the technical details here
