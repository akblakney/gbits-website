Request random bytes

Request random bytes from the hardware-based generator. Bytes are drawn from the generator from the last 60 seconds and are not retained after being served. Bytes that expire from the generator's pool are archived for statistical testing. The waveform plot shows the raw waveform audio plot from the microphone audio used to generate the bytes you request.
